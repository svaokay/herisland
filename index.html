Welcome to Eluriah!

hello! this is my first website, made in order to share all kinds of stuff about my visual novel, including the download, only when i will finish it! a visual novel game based on a particular view on existence and reality, that takes the story of a girl with a messed up mind and lets her find out the truth about the universe. three primordial creatures and a curiosity about what reality would be like, if experienced through the eyes of a mind unaware of the truth of existence the three creatures decide to create a new world. in a world where exors, powers born from the deepest experiences of a person, are secretely changing humanity, Swanpi is a silent child, excluded from others and unable to awaken her gift. but under the promise of a "better place", her father, the brilliant but dark scientist Takeno Aiden, drags her to a secret laboratory suspended in the sky.
